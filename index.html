Our mission

From the day we were founded, our mission here at Manzil Parwaaz has been simple. To help foster the alleviation of our community. Whether that has been through Social advocacy, or Enviromental sustainability we have remained true to our values of commitment and human dignity. Manzil Parwaaz has also found itself exploring different avenues of educational excellence, by indulging in activities ranging from STEM education to personal character development. We are proud at the difference we have made to this community, and strive to improve constantly.

Wings

Sabza

The earth as we know it has undergone terrible atrocities carried out by none other than mankind itself. Sabza emphasizes environmental sustainability through afforestation projects and awareness campaigns.

Taleem-o-Tarbiyat

Without education, a society will be socially politically, and economically backward This wing focuses on Educational Development, inclusive of adult literacy. We aim to prepare the less fortunate in our society through extensive sessions under this wing.

Roshan Khayali

This wing is directed towards promoting discourse and a greater understanding of social issues that affect the community at large. We want to promote social advocacy which is why we will be addressing various social concerns. We also plan to aware people of basic rights through vigorous and extensive sessions. We hope to break the social stigmas in our society for the betterment of humanity.

Roshnai

This wing will be employed to teach unprivileged students STEM education. Our aim is to shed light on the use of scientific methodologies for the community's welfare. Different aspects of Science will be incorporated into a curriculum designed to illuminate aspiring students who do not have the resources to study themselves.

Aasra

Hunger. The one driving force behind every stride a person makes everyday. To fill the ache in one's abdomen. Yet, at the same time, we see so much food being wasted, left to rot. Aasra aims to address this, to ease the pain of those who sleep on an empty belly. In cooperation with restaurants and eateries, Aasra will supply food packages containing the very leftovers we so mercilessly waste on a day to day basis, to those who truly need it.
